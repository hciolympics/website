5. The London Philharmonic Orchestra recorded 205 national anthems in just 52 hours.

6. 2012 marked the 30th Olympiad but only the 27th Summer Game. That is because 1916, 1940 and 1944 were all wartime Olympics, in which the games were canceled.

7. The gold medals are actually 93% silver and 1.3% gold.

8. Equestrian athlete Hiroshi Hoketsu, age 71 at the time, was the oldest competitor in the 2012 Games. Representing Japan, he has been competing since 1964 but has yet to medal.
